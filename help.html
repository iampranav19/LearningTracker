üìù Getting Started - Adding Your First Entry

Follow these simple steps to log your first learning session:

  1. 1Click the "Add Entry" button in the main navigation
  2. 2The date field is automatically set to today (you can change it if logging a past session)
  3. 3Enter the topic or subject you studied (e.g., "JavaScript Fundamentals", "Spanish Grammar")
  4. 4Select your learning type:

    Active Learning:

    Hands-on activities like coding, writing, practicing, problem-solving

    Passive Learning:

    Reading, watching videos, listening to lectures, reviewing notes

  5. 5Input time spent in hours (use decimals: 1.5 = 1 hour 30 minutes)
  6. 6Describe what you learned in the text area
  7. 7Add key takeaways as bullet points
  8. 8Click "Save Entry" to record your progress

üéØ Building Learning Streaks

  • Log at least one learning session each day to maintain your streak
  • Even 15 minutes of learning counts - consistency beats intensity
  • Use the calendar view to see your streak visually
  • Set realistic daily learning goals to maintain momentum
  • Don't break the chain - make learning a daily habit!

üí° Pro Tips for Success

  • Log immediately: Record your learning sessions right after completion for accuracy
  • Be specific: Use descriptive topic names for better tracking and searching
  • Use takeaways: Writing key points reinforces learning and creates a valuable reference
  • Consistency over quantity: 30 minutes daily beats 5 hours once a week
  • Regular exports: Back up your data monthly to prevent loss
  • Weekly reviews: Check your progress weekly to stay motivated and adjust goals
  • Mix learning types: Balance active and passive learning for optimal retention
  • Set mini-goals: Aim for small, achievable daily learning targets

üö® Troubleshooting

  • Data disappeared? Check if you cleared browser data or used incognito mode
  • Can't import file? Ensure the file is in Excel (.xlsx) or CSV format
  • Export not working? Check if pop-ups are blocked in your browser
  • Streak reset unexpectedly? Make sure you're logging entries with valid dates
  • Calendar not updating? Refresh the page or try adding a new entry
